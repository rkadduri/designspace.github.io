Requirement

To identify problems and/or opportunities in the existing mobile app and utilize our expertise to redesign and enhance the platform.

Objectives

  • Easy navigation through the application.
  • Discoverability i.e., effective ways to locate products with less effort.
  • Clear visuals and concise copy for communication.
  • Boost customer convenience and satisfaction.
  • Convey positive intent and constant support during and after the purchase to establish userâ€™s trust.

Problem

Client's industrial automation equipment marketplace faces issues with an overloaded, confusing interface, leading to dropouts and decreased sales due to monotonous promotions. The design lacks an ecommerce pattern, adding cognitive load for users.

Solution

Our design journey involved in-depth discussions with stakeholders and users, understanding business and user goals, collecting various personas, and studying competitors. We made significant improvements in workflow and user journey, introduced ecommerce design patterns, prioritized important actions, and used clean visuals, resulting in increased sales and reduced dropouts.

Discover & Define

The clutter in the app caused a cognitive load on the users. The research-led insights about user interaction with the app aided us to redefine the information architecture and user interface of the app. Analysed user journey and conducted contextual/competitive research to derive a clear understanding of how the ideal user interaction must be and ways to achieve it. Actively worked to receive every key stakeholder aligned with the redevelopment process and how it will impact the workflows.

Ideate, Design & Deliver.

During the brainstorming session, our team created wireframes and user flows to map out the user journey and test different design concepts.

Leveraged clear language and minimalistic visuals to make the app user-friendly and easy to navigate.
